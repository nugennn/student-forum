# Community Feature - Quick Start Guide

## üéØ What is the Community Feature?

The Community feature allows you to organize discussions into communities, making it easier to find relevant questions and engage with people interested in the same topics.

## üöÄ Getting Started

### 1. Create a Community

**Step 1:** Go to the Discussions page and click the **"+ Create Community"** button (green button next to "Ask Question")

**Step 2:** Fill in the community details:
- **Community Name** - Choose a unique, descriptive name (e.g., "Web Development", "Data Science")
- **Description** - Explain what your community is about and any guidelines
- **Icon** - Upload a community icon (optional, 200x200px recommended)
- **Banner** - Upload a community banner (optional, 1200x300px recommended)
- **Private Community** - Check if you want to restrict who can join

**Step 3:** Click **"Create Community"**

‚úÖ You're now the admin of your community!

---

### 2. Browse & Join Communities

**Step 1:** Click on **"Communities"** in the main navigation or go to `/community/`

**Step 2:** Browse available communities:
- See community cards with icons, names, and member counts
- Use the search bar to find specific communities
- Click **"View"** to see community details before joining

**Step 3:** Click **"Join"** on any community card

‚úÖ You're now a member! You'll see it in "My Communities"

---

### 3. Post a Question to a Community

**Step 1:** Click **"Ask Question"** button

**Step 2:** Fill in your question:
- **Title** - Clear, specific question title
- **Body** - Detailed question description
- **Tags** - Relevant tags

**Step 3:** Select a community (optional):
- Scroll down to find the **"Community (Optional)"** dropdown
- Select a community from your joined communities
- Leave blank to post without a community

**Step 4:** Click **"Review"** then **"Save Changes"**

‚úÖ Your question is now posted to the community!

---

### 4. View Community Details

**Step 1:** Click on any community name or card

**Step 2:** You'll see:
- Community icon and banner
- Description and guidelines
- Member count and creation date
- Recent members list
- Join/Leave button

**Step 3:** Click **"View all members ‚Üí"** to see all community members

---

### 5. Manage Your Communities

**View Your Communities:**
- Click **"My Communities"** in the sidebar
- See all communities you've joined
- Click any community to view details

**Leave a Community:**
- Go to community details page
- Click **"Leave Community"** button
- Confirm your choice

‚ö†Ô∏è **Note:** If you're the only admin, you can't leave the community

## üí° Tips & Best Practices

### Creating a Good Community

1. **Clear Name** - Use a descriptive name that clearly indicates the topic
   - ‚úÖ Good: "Python Programming", "Web Development", "Data Science"
   - ‚ùå Bad: "Cool Stuff", "Random", "Help"

2. **Detailed Description** - Explain:
   - What the community is about
   - Who should join
   - Community guidelines
   - What types of questions are welcome

3. **Visual Identity** - Add icon and banner:
   - Makes community recognizable
   - Encourages people to join
   - Looks more professional

4. **Active Engagement** - Keep community active:
   - Answer questions promptly
   - Welcome new members
   - Share relevant resources

### Joining Communities

1. **Browse First** - Check community details before joining
2. **Read Guidelines** - Understand community rules
3. **Participate** - Post questions and help others
4. **Be Respectful** - Follow community guidelines

## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: Can I create multiple communities?**
A: Yes! You can create as many communities as you want.

**Q: Can I join multiple communities?**
A: Yes! Join as many communities as you're interested in.

**Q: Can I change community settings after creating it?**
A: Yes! As an admin, you can edit community details (future feature).

**Q: What if I'm the only admin?**
A: You can't leave the community. You'll need to make someone else an admin first (future feature).

**Q: Can I delete a community?**
A: Yes, as the admin you can delete your community (future feature).

**Q: Are communities public or private?**
A: You can choose when creating. Public communities are visible to everyone. Private communities require approval to join.

**Q: Can I post without selecting a community?**
A: Yes! Community selection is optional. Your question will be posted without a community.

**Q: Can I move a question to a different community?**
A: Yes, you can edit your question and change the community (future feature).
